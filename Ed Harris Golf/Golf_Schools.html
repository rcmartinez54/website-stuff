Golf Schools

Half Day Golf School

Class is designed for newer players who want to get a better feel for the game of golf. Class fee is $325 and includes four hours of private instruction and covers the fundamentals of putting, chipping, pitching and the full swing. Everything the new player needs to get started.

One Day Golf School

Class is designed for beginner to intermediate players who want to develop their game to the next level. Spend four hours in the morning with your private instructor working on the four major shots in golf. After a nice lunch at the club lounge you'll now go to the course to play nine holes with your professional. This gives you an oppourtunity to apply all of the great things you learned that morning. Class fee is $595 and includes four hours of private instruction, lunch and play. Class covers putting, short game, full swing and course management. One of our most popular classes.

Two Day Golf School

By far our most popular class, it is designed for the serious player who wants to lower their handicap and have a better understanding of how to play the course in the most efficient manner. Each day, you will spend four hours with your instructor working on the major shots in golf. After lunch you and your professional will go to the course to work on application and course management. Fee is $995 and includes putting, chipping, pitching, distance wedges, bunkers and course management. Also includes a video evaluation of your golf swing.
