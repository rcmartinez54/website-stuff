Hello everyone! My name is Raymond Martinez and I love to play golf. I may not make the tour anytime soon, or ever, but one of my favorite things to do is tee it up. I recently moved to San Diego from San Jose and the golf here is amazing. My current home course, where I am a member of the Players Club, is Mission Trails GC which is located at the base of Cowles Mountain. It is hard not to play there because of the insane deal you get on green fees but I still get out and try new courses. My favorite course to play so far here in San Diego is Cornando Municipal GC (sorry Mission Trails, I love the ocean!).

Let us get into where I have played and where I plan on playing in the future.

Mission Trails GC

As mentioned above, this is my home course so I am very familiar with it. I can tell you exactly where all the hazards are because I've been in most of them. This is a photo from the first tee box. It is a 524 yard Par 5 from the whites with a slight dog-leg right. The fence to the left is the driving range so if you are a lefty and you slice, beware! Same goes for righties who push. To the right are four trees and a bunker. If you can hit a fairly straight drive for about 250 yards you wont havt to worry about neither the fence nor the bunker. From there you have a straight shot to the green which sits with about eight feet of elevation from the fairway. There is a drainage ditch running between you and the hole from this position so toppers be weary! I've definitely done it. To the right of the green is the second of the only two bunkers on the hole. The green runs back down towards the tee box.
